# Content Outline Templates

Use these templates to map extracted content into card-friendly sections.

## 1 Card (Short)
- Title
- 3-5 bullets
- 1 highlight quote/tag

## 2-3 Cards (Medium)
- Card 1: Cover (title + subtitle + 1-2 highlights)
- Card 2: Key points (4-6 bullets)
- Card 3: Tips/Steps or Summary + CTA

## 4-6 Cards (Long)
- Card 1: Cover
- Card 2: Key points (1)
- Card 3: Key points (2)
- Card 4: Steps/Checklist
- Card 5: Examples/Do-Don't
- Card 6: Summary + CTA

## Bullet Constraints
- 8-14 Chinese chars per line ideal.
- 3-7 bullets per card.
- Avoid paragraphs longer than 2 lines.

## CTA Ideas
- 记得收藏 / 关注了解更多
- 评论区聊聊你的看法
- 想要模板可以留言
